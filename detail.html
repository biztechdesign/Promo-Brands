The Aurora Insulated Bottle is built for brands that take sustainability seriously. Crafted from premium 18/8 grade stainless steel with a durable powder-coated finish, it’s designed to be used daily for years — not discarded after a week.

Double-wall vacuum insulation keeps drinks cold for up to 24 hours and hot for up to 12. The leak-proof lid means it travels safely in bags, on desks, and in backpacks without a second thought. A wide-mouth opening makes it easy to fill and clean.

Available in four brand-ready colours: Ink, White, Red, and Navy. Each can be decorated using four branding methods — printed, laser engraved, embossed, or debossed — giving you full creative control over the final result.
