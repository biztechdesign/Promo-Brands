Sustainability

Sustainability isn’t a range — it’s a default.

For most promotional merchandise, the lifetime is measured in weeks. We’re building a different kind of catalogue — products designed to be kept, used, and noticed long after the launch event has packed up.

That means recycled and organic materials wherever possible, suppliers we’ve audited in person, and an account team trained to recommend the lower-impact option first — not the cheapest one. We don’t greenwash. If a product can’t back up its eco-claim with evidence, we don’t list it as one.

  • GOTS, GRS & FSC certified ranges
  • BPMA Charter member since 2014
  • Carbon-balanced UK delivery
  • Plastic-free packaging by default

Four principles behind every product we list.

Eco-Friendly Materials

Recycled PET, organic cotton, FSC-certified paper, plant-based bioplastics — sourced from suppliers we’ve verified.

Responsible Sourcing

Audited supply chains, traceable origins and long-term partner relationships — never anonymous middlemen.

Reusable Products

Bottles, cups, totes and notebooks built to last — replacing single-use giveaways with lasting brand impressions.

Ethical Production

Fair-wage factories, safe working conditions and Sedex-monitored partners across our European and overseas supply base.
