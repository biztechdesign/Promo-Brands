Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Can’t find what you’re looking for? Drop us a line.

What’s the minimum order quantity?

MOQs vary by product — some start as low as 25 units (notebooks, badges, USB drives), others from 100 (totes, drinkware) or 250 (apparel). Tell us what you’re after and we’ll quote on the lowest viable quantity.

How long does production take?

Standard turnaround is 10–15 working days from artwork sign-off, including UK delivery. Express options are available for most products at 5–7 working days for an additional charge.

Can I order a sample before committing?

Absolutely — we offer plain samples of most products for the cost of postage. Branded pre-production samples are available on larger orders to confirm print position and colour before the full run.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship worldwide via fully tracked, insured couriers. Multi-destination orders (e.g. one delivery per regional office) are quoted at the same time as the production cost.

What artwork formats do you accept?

Vector files preferred (AI, EPS, PDF with editable type). High-resolution PNG or JPEG (300dpi+) work for full-colour digital prints. If you only have a low-res file, our studio can re-draw your logo at no charge for orders over £500.

Do you offer eco-friendly products?

Yes — most catalogue lines have a verified eco alternative. See our Sustainability page for our approach to materials, sourcing and packaging.
